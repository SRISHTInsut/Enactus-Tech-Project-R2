Enactus NSUT - Driving Social Change

Enactus NSUT is dedicated to empowering communities through social entrepreneurship. By addressing real-world issues with innovative, sustainable solutions, it promotes social and economic upliftment. Each project nurtures student leadership while fostering significant societal impact, reflecting NSUT's commitment to meaningful change.

Agni

Upcycling for Empowerment

Agni repurposes retired fire hoses into products like bags and pouches, empowering women entrepreneurs and prisoners.

Annotsav

Empowering Farmers Through Innovation

Annotsav helps farmers overcome high costs and low productivity using sustainable practices, boosting farm efficiency and yields.

Clair

Clean Air for Every Household

Clair makes fresh air affordable with innovative air purification systems designed for lower and middle-class communities.

Urja

Harnessing Dormant Wind Energy

Urja focuses on repurposing unused wind energy, enhancing upcycled products while empowering communities.
